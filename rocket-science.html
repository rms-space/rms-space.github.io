Rocket Science

Rocket propulsion works on Newton's Third Law of Motion: For every action there is an equal and opposite reaction.

Rocket Thrust Equation

F = ṁVe + (Pe − Pa)Ae

  • F = Thrust
  • ṁ = Mass flow rate
  • Ve = Exhaust velocity
  • Pe = Exit pressure
  • Pa = Ambient pressure
  • Ae = Nozzle exit area

Rocket Equation

Δv = Ve ln(m₀ / m_f)

  • Δv = Change in velocity
  • Ve = Exhaust velocity
  • m₀ = Initial mass
  • m_f = Final mass
