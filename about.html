About RMS Space

RMS Space is an aerospace knowledge platform dedicated to rockets, missile technology, satellites and propulsion systems.

The website aims to provide educational content, technical explanations and research insights related to aerospace engineering and space technology.

Research Interests

  • Rocket propulsion systems
  • Electrically Controlled Solid Propellants (ECSP)
  • Combustion behaviour of solid propellants
  • Ignition delay analysis
  • Rocket motor ballistics
