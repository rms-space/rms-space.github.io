News & Events

This section covers recent developments in space technology, rocket launches, satellite missions and defense systems. It provides a structured overview of key advancements in aerospace engineering.

🚀 Space Missions

Gaganyaan Human Spaceflight Program

India’s Gaganyaan mission aims to send astronauts into Low Earth Orbit using an indigenous launch system, marking a major milestone in human spaceflight capability.

Artemis Lunar Missions

NASA’s Artemis program focuses on returning humans to the Moon and establishing a sustainable presence for future deep space exploration.

🛰 Rocket Launches

Reusable Launch Vehicles

Modern launch systems focus on reusability to reduce cost per launch. Reusable boosters significantly improve launch economics and sustainability.

Heavy Lift Launch Vehicles

Heavy-lift rockets are being developed to carry large payloads for deep space missions, satellite constellations and interplanetary travel.

📡 Satellite Deployments

Satellite Constellations

Large constellations of satellites are being deployed in Low Earth Orbit to provide global internet coverage and communication services.

Earth Observation Satellites

Advanced imaging satellites are used for climate monitoring, disaster management and environmental analysis.

🎯 Defense Technology

Hypersonic Missile Systems

Hypersonic vehicles capable of speeds greater than Mach 5 are being developed worldwide, offering high maneuverability and reduced interception capability.

Advanced Guidance Systems

Modern missile systems use inertial navigation, GPS guidance and advanced seekers such as radar and infrared for precise targeting.
