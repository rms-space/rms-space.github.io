Research Work

Electrically Controlled Solid Propellants (ECSP)

This research focuses on electrically controlled ignition and combustion behaviour of solid propellants for advanced propulsion systems.

Research Topics

  • Ignition delay experiments
  • Voltage vs burn rate studies
  • Re-ignition experiments
  • Temperature measurement using IR camera
  • Lithium Perchlorate solubility analysis
