Research Work

This section presents research interests and ongoing studies related to rocket propulsion, combustion behaviour and advanced solid propellant technologies.

Research Interests

  • Rocket motor ballistics
  • Rocket propulsion systems
  • Combustion behaviour of solid propellants
  • Computational methods and numerical analysis

Electrically Controlled Solid Propellants (ECSP)

Current research focuses on electrically controlled ignition and combustion behaviour of solid propellants for advanced propulsion systems.

Research Topics

  • Ignition delay experiments
  • Voltage vs burn rate studies
  • Re-ignition experiments
  • Temperature measurement using IR camera
  • Lithium Perchlorate solubility analysis
