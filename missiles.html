Missile Technology

Missiles are guided weapon systems designed to deliver payloads to specific targets with high accuracy.

Types of Missiles

  • Ballistic Missiles
  • Cruise Missiles
  • Surface-to-Air Missiles
  • Anti-Ship Missiles

Guidance Systems

Modern missiles use advanced guidance systems such as:

  • Inertial Navigation Systems (INS)
  • GPS Guidance
  • Radar Homing
  • Infrared Homing
