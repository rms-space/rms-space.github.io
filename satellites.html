Satellite Systems

Satellites are spacecraft placed in orbit around Earth or other celestial bodies to perform communication, navigation, observation and scientific missions.

Types of Orbits

  • Low Earth Orbit (LEO)
  • Medium Earth Orbit (MEO)
  • Geostationary Orbit (GEO)

Satellite Applications

  • Communication
  • Earth Observation
  • Navigation (GPS)
  • Scientific Research
